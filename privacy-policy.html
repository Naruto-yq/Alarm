1. 概述

LiJuan Yang（以下简称「我们」）尊重你的隐私。本政策说明 iOS 应用 快捷闹钟（以下简称「本应用」）如何收集、使用与保护信息。 使用本应用即表示你理解并同意本政策；若不同意，请停止使用本应用。

2. 我们收集哪些信息

2.1 你主动提供或在本机生成的内容

  • 闹钟与提醒相关数据：例如闹钟时间、重复规则、标签、铃声选择等，用于提供闹钟与提醒功能。
  • 自定义铃声文件：若你导入音频作为铃声，文件保存在设备本地存储中，用于在闹钟触发时播放。

上述数据默认存储在你的设备上，我们不会为实现上述功能而要求你注册账户，也不在本政策所述范围外主动将上述内容上传至我们运营的服务器。

2.2 设备权限与系统能力

  • 定位（使用期间）：若你使用天气相关功能，我们可能在获得授权后读取设备位置，用于向天气数据服务请求你所在区域的预报信息。
  • 通知与闹钟（含系统闹钟接口）：用于在约定时间响铃、贪睡与停止等；相关调度主要发生在系统与设备本地。

2.3 广告与衡量（免费用户可能适用）

若你未订阅去除广告的权益，本应用可能通过 Google Mobile Ads（AdMob） 展示广告。 Google 及其合作伙伴可能使用设备标识符、广告标识符、IP 地址、大致位置、应用交互等信息，用于展示与衡量广告、防欺诈及改进服务。 详见 Google 的说明：Google 隐私权政策。 本应用在 Info.plist 中声明了与广告归因相关的 SKAdNetwork 标识符，以符合 Apple 平台要求。

2.4 订阅与购买

若你购买订阅或应用内项目，交易由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理。 我们可能通过 Apple 提供的机制获知订阅是否有效等状态，用于解锁相应功能；我们不会通过本应用直接收集你的银行卡号。

2.5 本地设置与诊断

1. Overview

LiJuan Yang (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the iOS app Quick Alarm (the “App”) handles information. By using the App, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the App.

2. Information we process

2.1 Content you create or that is stored on device

  • Alarm and reminder data (e.g. time, repeat rules, labels, sound choices) to provide alarms and reminders.
  • Custom sound files you import may be stored locally to play when an alarm fires.

This data is stored on your device by default. We do not require an account for these features and do not upload this content to servers we operate except as described here.

2.2 Permissions and system features

  • Location (when in use): If you use weather features, we may access location after your permission to request forecast data for your area.
  • Notifications and alarms (including system alarm APIs): Used to fire alarms, snooze, and stop; scheduling occurs primarily on-device and via the system.

2.3 Advertising and measurement (may apply to free users)

If you do not have an active subscription that removes ads, the App may show ads through Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). Google and its partners may use identifiers, IP address, coarse location, and app interaction data for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and service improvement. See Google’s Privacy Policy. The App declares SKAdNetwork identifiers in Info.plist as required on Apple platforms.

2.4 Subscriptions and purchases

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple via the App Store. We may rely on Apple-provided signals (e.g. subscription status) to unlock features. We do not collect your payment card number through the App.

2.5 Local settings

We may store preferences and ad frequency-related settings locally (e.g. via UserDefaults). The App does not integrate standalone third-party analytics SDKs unless listed below; system logging is for development diagnostics and not used for cross-app tracking.

3. Third-party services

  • Google Mobile Ads: ad serving and related technology; subject to Google’s terms and privacy policy.
  • Apple (App Store / StoreKit): payment processing and subscription status.
  • Weather data providers: We may call third-party weather APIs (e.g. Open-Meteo). Requests may include coordinates or region derived from location you authorize, as needed to return forecasts.

4. Networking and storage

  • Weather and ads may be transmitted over encrypted networks (HTTPS).
